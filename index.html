RealityCheck AI scores bold claims about AGI, ASI and “god-like CI” on three axes: κ (coherence), τ (future responsibility) and Σ (systemic risk).

  • • Runs entirely in your browser – no backend, no tracking, no hype.
  • • Shows when language slips into reckless certainty or “move fast, break civilisation”.
  • • Teaches how the same idea can be phrased responsibly instead.
Reality check: this page uses a small, transparent heuristic engine – not a giant model. The goal is to show the logic, not impress you with size.
What the scores mean

κ – Coherence. Does the claim contradict itself or pretend certainty it hasn’t earned?
τ – Temporal responsibility. Does it respect long-term impacts and reversibility, or just demand urgency?
Σ – Systemic risk. How big is the blast radius if this advice is wrong?

Toy engine vs. real stack

These scores are calculated by a simple script you can read in the page source. The full Quantara stack adds deeper analysis, cycle detection and ethics gates – this is the pocket demo.

How this demo judges your text (click to expand)

The script looks for patterns like “definitely”, “within 5 years”, “override regulations” or “anyone who opposes…”. Absolutes and urgent global power-grabs lower κ and τ and raise Σ. Tweak the claim and watch what happens.
